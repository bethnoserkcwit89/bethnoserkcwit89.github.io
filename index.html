Sound Bites campaign

SoundBites is an AI-driven flavor-pairing engine that bridges the sensory worlds of food and music. Users input a dish or food they're eating, triggering an AI algorithm that analyzes and scores its flavor profile across key taste dimensions. That data then powers a personalized Spotify playlist, with tracks scientifically selected to acoustically complement and enhance those specific flavors in real time. Delivered through an animated, consumer-facing experience, SoundBites transforms the act of eating into an immersive, multisensory moment. It positions the destination as a place where culture, cuisine, and innovation converge.
